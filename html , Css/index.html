Dillworth Capital seeks to
acquire, operate and grow a
single small to medium-sized,
privately held business.

OUR GOALS

To facilitate an appealing exit option for the entrepreneur and owner. Our goal is to preserve the culture that has been created, continue growing the company, and take an active role in operating the business while ensuring a seamless transition that minimizes distractions and maintains continuity for all parties.

OUR FOCUS

We are focused on buying just one business. We are not distracted with a portfolio of businesses. 100% of our attention, resources, and capital will go into acquiring and operating the business. Our long-term focus is growing the business and taking the next step in the evolution of the company.

OUR INTENTIOS

We want the acquisition process to be as simple and smooth as possible. No drawn out delays, complicated or ambiguous terms, and no financial engineering. We are flexible on deal terms and structure. Small businesses are a family of employees and our plan is to keep the family intact.
